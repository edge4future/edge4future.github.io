Welcome to Edge4Future

The First International Workshop on Edge Systems for Future Mobile Networks

Edge4Future aims to be an international forum for the exchange of knowledge and experience between researchers and industrials working on the development of solutions linked to Edge Computing, which today occupies an important place in future communications networks.

In 2024, Edge4Future will be organized in Paris in conjunction with the 20th IEEE WiMob conference the 9th of October.

Edge4Future encourages the submission of original works describing research results, practical or industrial edge systems/solutions/frameworks. Papers describing advanced prototypes, platforms, techniques and general survey for discussing future perspectives and directions are also encouraged.

Important dates

  • Paper submission: June 24, 2024
  • Acceptance notification: August 19, 2024
  • Camera-ready Submission: September 9, 2024
  • Edge4Future Event: October 9, 2024
