Authors guidelines

All submitted papers should describe original work and not be published or under review anywhere. The papers will be judged on the basis of their clarity, relevance, originality, and contribution.

All accepted papers will be published in the IEEE WiMob conference proceedings, and will be submitted for inclusion in IEEE Digital Xplore.

Authors are required to submit fully formatted papers (PDF), with graphs, images, and other special areas arranged as intended for the final publication. Papers should be written in English conforming to the IEEE standard conference format (8.5" x 11" - US letter or A4, Two-Column). The final manuscript for publication will be limited to six (6) pages (maximum of two (2) additional pages can be purchased, each paper cannot exceed eight (8) pages). IEEE standard conference template for Microsoft Word or LaTeX formats can be found here. Your paper must be printable in order to be accepted.

At least one author of each accepted paper must register at the regular rate (non-student) at IEEE WiMob conference (single package that includes both the main conference and workshop sessions) and present the paper during the event, otherwise the paper will not be included in the proceedings

More information regarding authors guidelines can be found directly on the WiMob Author Guidelines Page.
